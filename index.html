A ThreadPoolExecutor Deep-Dive

March 19, 2020

We provide a motivation for the introduction of the ThreadPoolExecutor and Executors classes in JDK 1.5. We describe how to create fixed and cached thread-pools through Executors's factory methods, and then how to create thread-pools by manual instantiation of the ThreadPoolExecutor class. We finally proceed to dig into the implementation of ThreadPoolExecutor, raising warnings on the behaviors experienced by uncareful selection of instatiation parameters.
